Baked Macaroni & Cheese

Ingredients

  • 32oz Box of Macaroni and Cheese
  • 1 1/2 stick of butter
  • 3 cups of Mild Cheddar Cheese
  • 6 cups of Finely Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 bag of Monterary Cheddar Cubed Cheese
  • 2 cups of Milk
  • 2 cups of Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1 1/2 cups of Half and Half
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 4 eggs
  • Knorr chicken powder bouillon cube powder
  • Season Mix: peppercorn meldley grinder, iodized salt, garlic powder, onion powder, ground black pepper, smoked paprika

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Grease large size pan with 1 stick of butter (not margarine y’al), and set aside
  3. Put water into a large pot and sprinkle Knorr chicken boullion cube powder into water
  4. Partially cook elbow macaroni for 6-7 minuntes
  5. While pasta is cooking make a wet mix of finely shredded cheddar cheese, milk, heavy whipping cream, half and half, and eggs. Lightly season with season mix listed above
  6. Once pasta is partially cooked mix with half stick of butter. Lightly season with season mix listed above.
  7. Combine wet mix, seasoned pasta, and bag of cubed cheese into bowl and mix throughly
  8. Pour mix and spread Into large size pan
  9. Sprinkle mild cheddar cheeder cheese on top of macaroni mix
  10. Bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour until cheese creates a dark golden brown crust
Enjoy!

Collard Greens

Ingredients

  • 4 bags or bunches of collard greens
  • 2 pounds of smoked turkey wings or necks
  • 10 cloves whole cloves of garlic
  • 3 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • 1 bag of frozen onion and pepper mix
  • 1 tablespoon of hot sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoon sugar
  • Splash of white distilled vinegar or apple cider vinegar
  • Season Mix: Cajun choice blackened seasonings, Knorr chicken boullion cube powder, crushed red pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, thyme leaves, salt, peppercorn medley grinder

Instructions

  1. Place smoked turkey meat with a little sprinkle of knorrs seasoning in a pot with water
  2. Allow to boil on medium heat for atleast 45 minutes or enough for meat to soften
  3. While smoke turkey is cooking throughly wash collard greens in a bowl then dump in a pot
  4. Add season mix, minced garlic, garlic cloves, onion, hot sauce, sugar, and your choice of vinegar in a pot
  5. Allow to cook for 30 minutes to allow greens to steam and become smaller
  6. Mix smoked turkey into greens and add more water
  7. Now allow for greens and smoked turkey to simmer together on medium heat for another 30 minutes.
  8. Let sit for 10 minutes before serving
Enjoy!

Candied Yams

Ingredients

  • 10 medium size sweet potatoes
  • 2 stick of butter
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tsp ground all spice
  • Butter cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven 350
  2. Wash sweet potatoes in warm water
  3. Place washed sweet potatoes in a large pot with water allow for potatoes to come to a boil
  4. Don’t allow sweet potatoes to completely soften while boiling, only just enough to stick a knife 1/2 through
  5. Drain, and allow to cool then take off skin
  6. Cut sweet potaoes and place in a large long pan that is sprayed with butter cooking spray and set aside
  7. Melt butter, brown sugar, and sugar on low to medium heat to create a sugar mix
  8. Pour sugar mix into sweet potatoes.
  9. Season sweet potatoes and sugar mix with cinnamon, nutmeg, and ground all spice
  10. Mix sugar mix and sweet potatoes in a bowl
  11. Pour candied yams in large bake dish add 1 1/2 cups of water and cover with foil
  12. Allow yams to cook for 35-45 minutes or until yams have softened
Enjoy!

Crockpot Turkey Wings

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of Turkey Wings
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 1 bag of frozen peppers and onion mix
  • 7 cloves of garlic
  • 1 large white onion
  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • 1 Lipton onion soup mix
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 4 cups of water
  • Seasoning mix of natures seasoning, Italian seasoning, Tony
  • Chacheres more spice, salt, ground black pepper, onionpowder. ( 1/2 teaspoon of each)

Instructions

  1. Throughly wash turkey wings with water and lemon juice in bowl
  2. Combine turkey wings, seasoning mix, fresh garlic cloves, chopped white onion, minced garlic, peppers and onion frozen mix, butter, onion soup mix and olive oil in crockpot.
  3. Stir ingredients togeter until turkey wings are completely coated with seasoning
  4. Mix 2 tablespoons of cornstarch with water in a measuring up and pour Into crock pot.
  5. Pour in 2 cups of cold water.
  6. Allow to cook in croc pot throughout the day on high for 3-4 hours.
  7. Periodically check and add water until meat has fallen off the bone.
Enjoy with a side of your choice!

Banana Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 box of vanilla wafers
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 6 sliced bannas thin
  • 11/2 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 large container of whipped cream

Instructions

  • Crush up vanilla wafers in a bag, set aside a little for decoration
  • Melt stick of butter them combine with wafers
  • Mix wafers and butter in bowl and place at the bottom of large container to make a crust and set aside
  • Mix together sliced bannas and lemon juice in a bowl and spread on top of wafer crust
  • Pour entire can of condensed milk on top of bananas
  • Cover with whip cream and sprinkle extra crushed wafers on top
  • Let chill 4-5 hours or overnight for best results
    • Enjoy!
