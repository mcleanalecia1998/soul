Banana Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 box of vanilla wafers
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 6 sliced bannas thin
  • 11/2 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 large container of whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Crush up vanilla wafers in a bag, set aside a little for decoration
  2. Melt stick of butter them combine with wafers
  3. Mix wafers and butter in bowl and place at the bottom of large container to make a crust and set aside
  4. Mix together sliced bannas and lemon juice in a bowl and spread on top of wafer crust
  5. Pour entire can of condensed milk on top of bananas
  6. Cover with whip cream and sprinkle extra crushed wafers on top
  7. Let chill 4-5 hours or overnight for best results

Enjoy!
